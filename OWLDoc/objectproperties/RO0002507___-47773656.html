Object Property: 'determined by'

http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/RO_0002507

Annotations (10)

  • 'example of usage' "A coral reef environment is determined by a particular coral reef" ()
  • definition "s determined by f if and only if s is a type of system, and f is a material entity that is part of s, such that f exerts a strong causal influence on the functioning of s, and the removal of f would cause the collapse of s." ()
  • 'editor note' "The label for this relation is probably too general for its restricted use, where the domain is a system. It may be relabeled in future" ()

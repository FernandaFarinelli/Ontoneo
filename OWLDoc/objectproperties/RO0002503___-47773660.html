Object Property: towards

http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/RO_0002503

Annotations (5)

  • definition "q towards e2 if and only if q is a relational quality such that q inheres-in some e, and e != e2 and q is dependent on e2" ()
  • 'editor note' "This relation is provided in order to support the use of relational qualities such as 'concentration of'; for example, the concentration of C in V is a quality that inheres in V, but pertains to C." ()
  • 'term editor' "Chris Mungall" ()
  • 'definition source' http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/20064205
  • label "towards" ()

Superproperties (1)

  • 'depends on'

Usage (4)

  • ObjectProperty: towards
  • 'Inguinal hernia' ≡ 'has part' some ('herniated into' and 'inheres in' some 'Wall of abdomen' and towards some 'Inguinal ring' and 'has modifier' some abnormal)
  • 'blood pressure' ≡ pressure and towards some 'Wall of blood vessel' and 'inheres in' only ('Portion of blood' and 'participates in' some 'blood circulation')
  • towards ⊆ 'depends on'
